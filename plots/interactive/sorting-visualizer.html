🤔 Algorithm Comparison

Bubble Sort: Simple but slow O(n²). Good for learning.

Selection Sort: Also O(n²) but minimizes swaps.

Insertion Sort: O(n²) but fast on nearly sorted data.

Merge Sort: O(n log n), stable, uses extra memory.

Quick Sort: O(n log n) average, in-place, fast in practice.
