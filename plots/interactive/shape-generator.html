🤔 Geometric Concepts

Circle: Perfectly round shape, all points equidistant from center.

Star: Created by connecting points around a circle at specific angles.

Polygon: Many-sided shape, can be regular (equal sides) or irregular.

Rotation: Transforming a shape around a center point by a certain angle.
