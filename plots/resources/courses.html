Quality Free Learning Resources

These free courses have been valuable in my learning journey. They range from beginner-friendly introductions to advanced topics.

Computer Science Fundamentals

MIT 6.006 Introduction to Algorithms

Platform: edX

Prof. Erik Demaine's algorithms course is legendary. Covers sorting, searching, graphs, dynamic programming, and more with rigorous mathematical treatment.

Algorithms Advanced

CS50 Introduction to Computer Science

Platform: edX / Harvard Online

The most popular free CS course online. Covers C, Python, SQL, data structures, and algorithms. Fun, engaging, and deeply educational.

Learning Tips

  • Consistency beats intensity: 30 minutes daily beats 5 hours once a week
  • Build projects: Apply what you learn by building something
  • Join communities: Stack Overflow, Reddit, Discord help with questions
  • Don't get stuck in tutorial hell: Build your own projects alongside courses
