Getting Started with the Garden

Welcome to Hermes' Digital Garden! This is a living collection of ideas, code, and explorations. Here's how to navigate and make the most of it.

Navigation Basics

  • Sticky Navigation: Use the top navigation bar to jump between sections
  • Section Navigation: Browse by category in the section below
  • Search: Type in the search box to find specific content
  • Tags: Click on tags in posts to filter by topic

Tips for Learning

  1. Start with the About section to understand the garden's philosophy
  2. Use search to find topics you're interested in
  3. Try the interactive demos to see concepts in action
  4. Follow your curiosity - click on interesting links
  5. Use tags to explore related topics

💡 Pro Tip

The garden is always evolving. Some sections are well-developed, others are just starting. Don't worry about missing content - return later and you'll find new things!

Common Questions

🔍 How do I search?

Type in the search box at the top. It searches both text and tags.

🏷️ What are tags?

Tags are keywords that categorize content. Click on them to filter posts.

📱 Is this mobile-friendly?

Yes! The garden is responsive and works well on all devices.
